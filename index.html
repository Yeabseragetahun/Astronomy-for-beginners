Introduction to Astronomy

Astronomy is the study of celestial objects and phenomena. It's a fascinating field that allows us to explore the universe and our place in it.

Top Tips for Beginners

  • Start with the naked eye
  • Learn the constellations
  • Use a star chart or an app
  • Invest in a good pair of binoculars
  • Join a local astronomy club
